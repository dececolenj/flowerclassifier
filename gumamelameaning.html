What is a gumamela?

The gumamela flowers come in a wide variety of colors such as red, pink, yellow, orange, white, and even bi-colored. They are large, showy, and often have a prominent trumpet-like structure in the center. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, and the flowers bloom year-round in tropical regions.

Gumamela is also known for its medicinal properties. The flowers, leaves, and roots have been used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of ailments, including respiratory infections, stomach problems, and high blood pressure. The plant is also used in cosmetic and hair care products due to its soothing and hydrating properties.
